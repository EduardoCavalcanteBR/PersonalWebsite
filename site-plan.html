Overview

Purpose

The main objective of this site is to generate content, show a portfolio of previous works and attract potential clients who want to get married. I will do this by clearing doubts about how the work works and showing the quality and differentials of the service through photos and videos, in this way I will make my business more active, generate more revenue and, above all, deliver a special record of one of the most important moments in the life of a couple.

Audience

The Site is aimed at all people who are interested in getting married and want to understand a little more about the service of a filmmaker.

Content

Home page

The home page aims to show the work of a filmmaker, the first impact, and show how important it is to have a quality professional, capable of recording such an important moment that is the Wedding, in this part we will show a little about some records and solving questions like, "is it worth hiring a filmmaker?" "Is this professional capable of providing us with beautiful and striking records?" We will also provide you with contact information and then a personalized quote.

Portfolio

Kazzei Films specializes in storytelling through videos and recording key moments in people's lives. Photo and video go far beyond just recording an occasion.
