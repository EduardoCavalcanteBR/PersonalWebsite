About us

Kazzei Films specializes in storytelling through videos and recording key moments in people's lives. Photo and video go far beyond just recording an occasion. The phrase of the Brazilian poet, translator and journalist Mário Quintana “The photographer has the same mission as the poet: to immortalize the passing moment.” And we already know that photos and videos are the most important ways to immortalize feelings, experiences and achievements. It is through photos and videos that we can remember moments and keep forever moments that words cannot describe, after all, every record brings with it the key to unique and special memories. Everything in life is based on memory. Who we are, who we know, how we live, how we feel. Therefore, it is important to remember these moments, to remember what is good. All these stages are responsible for part of the construction of our self. It is important to keep in mind what we have lived through, who we know, what we were, how we grew up. Photos and videos allow us to look into the past to relive it all over again. Registering achievements, moments and stages of life is essential, can you imagine its importance in an event, such as a wedding? That's why Kazzei Films is totally focused on delivering the best service to the bride and groom, taking care to record every little detail. In addition, seeing what needs to be kept from that moment, showing the feelings of the moment in each click: emotion, anxiety, love and happiness. Be sure to remember this moment that is so important in your life as a couple, immortalize what is good for you to celebrate this stage you experienced in the future.

Guh Cavalcante

An enthusiast, passionate about recording remarkable and unique moments. Born in Curitiba, Brazil and founder of Kazzei Films, he aims to immortalize the most important moments in people's lives.
